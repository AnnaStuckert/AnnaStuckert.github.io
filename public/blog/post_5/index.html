DeepLabCut AI Residency

The DLC AI residency program is aimed at providing training opportunities for under-represented groups computer science in order to  democratize AI-based behavioral analysis in life sciences, with the ultimate goal of making open-science tools enabling quantitative insights in behavior analysis. I was a resident in 2024, feel free to reach out with any questions.
